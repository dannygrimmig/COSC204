Debate: Banning Conspiracy Theorist from Social Media

Yes

  • Social Media Core Values: "Community first, inspire creativity"
  • Marginalization of Opinions:
  • Mistrust: Misrepresentation of human social world, why alow these ideas on a social platform

No

  • What is a conspiracy?:Who gets to decide, how does this effect free speech
  • Validity: Things that are labeled conspiracy, may turn out to be true
  • Echo Chambers:Banning certian content may lead to diversion of opinions
  • Move Platforms: Conspiracy Theorists will move to unregulated platforms
    • Counter: At least now you have the choice to go onto this platform... rather than just seeing it on your feed

Key Takeaways

  • Questionable Content Flag: Not banning but just a warning
  • Productive Speech: How do we encourage productive speech, are these platforms designed for this?
  • Does Truth Win?: Deeper ideological questiion, do we believe in citizens to make the right choices or not?
