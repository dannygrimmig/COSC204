Guidlines for Ethics in a study domain

Respect for Persons

  • Protecting the autonomy of all people and treating them with courtesy and respect; informed consent; conducting non deception

Beneficence

  • Do no harm; maximize benefits for the project while minimizing risks to participants

Justice

  • Reasonable, non-exploitative, and well-considered procedures are administered fairly

Association for Computing MAchinery (ACM)

Contribute to society and to human well being, acknowledgin that all people are stakeholders in computing

  • Not only should we act ethically as an individual, but also collectively
  • Promoting fundamental human rights and protecting each individual's right to autonomy
  • Minimize negative consequences

Avoid Harm

  • Begins with careful consideration of potential impacts on all those affected by decisions
  • When harm is unintentional, those responsible are obliged to undo or mitigate harm as much as possible
  • When harm is intentional, those responsible are obligatetd to ensure harm is ethically justified.
  • Obligation to report signs of system risks
