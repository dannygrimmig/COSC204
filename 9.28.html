Fingers on the Scale

As long as we, humans, faavor certain kinds of speech, and as long as this medium runs on algorithms designed to give us more of the content thata will keep us engaged, then the medium will always have fingers on the scale

This is a key difference between online platforms and physical public forums: Online platforms choose what information to presesnt to you, and they do not do it randomly or without intention.

The way they serve information is motivated by corporate goal, which if it has an impact on discourse then it is no longer neutral.
