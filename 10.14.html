Debate: Should the Internet be a Human Right?

Yes

  • Access to Internet and its Attatched Rights: restricting the access to internet restricts other rights.
  • The UN: declared internet as a human right
  • Active Participant: social security, credit/debit, finances
  • The UN: declared internet as a human right
  • Implied rights: declared internet as a human right
  • Right to Education: educative element to internet
  • Flip the Switch: imagine they flipped a switch and all of a sudden you had no food... same with internet. money, education, lifestyle (syria cazectstan)

No

  • Absolute Human Rights: cant limit them... cant sensor the internet... who makes those decisions (legal can of worms)
  • Dangerous Precedent: cant equate internet to food and water (essentials)
  • In Person Alternatives: faster on the internet...
    • Counter: if this is acknowledged, this can be unfair and cause diversion (learning of crisis, money)
  • Enforcement: Hard to enforce
  • Technological Solutionism: would this really fix poverty and sistemic racism? Probably not.
  • UN: UN declared this a human right but have not actually done anything!
