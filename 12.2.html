Debate: The Metaverse

The Metaverse is good

our ideas
  • Connectivity Across Distance: spend time with others who are far (like social media but in real time)
  • Equality: people living hard lives in the real world can escape to the metaverse
  • Innovation: is the internet good or bad, who knows... regardless it is innovation

The Metaverse is not good

other sides ideas
  • Presumptious: dont know enough about it
  • Legal Policy: how do we handle crimes, who decides this?
  • Theft: online identity theft
  • Privacy: increased bodily tracking, increased data collected on humans
  • Mental Health: combine health issues with life and video games... bad combo
  • Barriers to Entry: it is expensive

Key Takeaways

The Metaverse: "It is the internet, but it's 3D"
