Napster

Computers are many purpose machinces, they are logically malleable

  • Unlimited uses for computers, unlike a refrigerator

Computers generate new possibilities for human action

  • These new possibilities may generate policy vacuums or instances in which there is no policy or law to protect us from negative outcomes
    • Policy vacuums are accompanied (or caused) by conceptual muddles or a lack of clarity around new concepts
